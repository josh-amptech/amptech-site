## AmpTech in one line
AmpTech helps you take your AI project from prototype to production. Senior engineering. Precise. Controlled power. Never hypey.

## Key files
- `README.md` — full brand context, content fundamentals, visual foundations, iconography
- `colors_and_type.css` — all design tokens + semantic type classes. Start here in any HTML artifact.
- `assets/` — logos (horizontal, stacked, mark-only, across light/dark/transparent)
- `ui_kits/marketing/` — high-fidelity marketing-site components you can lift
- `preview/` — small cards demoing each token / component in isolation

## Non-negotiables
- Brand red is `#D41E10` (R212 G30 B16 · C14 M100 Y100 K0) — official brand value. Accent only; never a giant background.
- Typography: **Space Grotesk** (display), **Inter** (body), **JetBrains Mono** (metrics/code) — official brand fonts, all via Google Fonts.
- No emoji. No hype words. No smiling-team stock photography. No bluish-purple gradients.
- Radii stay small (2–14px); the brand is angular, not pillowy.
- Voice = senior engineer at a whiteboard. "You" is the founder; "we" is AmpTech.
